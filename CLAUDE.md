# SorgAI Project Notes

## About the Project
Moldova-based team with a unique sorghum variety that is 3–5× more efficient in biomass, growth, climate resilience, and soil resilience. Science and experiment-proven, including white papers and world-renowned agricultural scientists. Goal: promote it worldwide via a web application, promotional materials, scientific white papers, and AI models/software to help agricultural companies grow it more efficiently.

## Website Requirements
- **Bilingual**: All website content must be available in both **Romanian** and **English**.
- **Responsive**: All web pages, components, and HTML files must be fully responsive and work on phones, tablets, and desktops. Always include `@media` breakpoints for at least: ≤900px (tablet), ≤600px (mobile), ≤380px (small phone). On mobile: collapse nav to hamburger menu, stack grids to 1–2 columns, reduce padding, make buttons full-width.
- Target audiences: farmers, food companies, investors, energy companies, agricultural businesses worldwide.

## Key Scientific Facts (from 425-page Romanian monograph)
- Green biomass yield: 80–100 t/ha (2–3× more than maize silage)
- CO₂ absorbed: 50–55 t/ha/year (3× more than a deciduous forest)
- Bioethanol from stalks: 4,152–9,157 L/ha (2× more than maize)
- Syrup antioxidant power: 4× that of honey
- Protein in grain: 10.6% (vs. 8.7% in maize)
- Non-GMO, gluten-free, low glycemic index
- Soil pH tolerance: 5.0–8.5
